Learning Objective

Introduction

Searching and sorting algorithms are essential in computer science, allowing efficient data retrieval and organization. They enable finding specific elements and arranging data in a desired order, serving as fundamental tools for numerous applications.

Duration: 8 Hours

Focus: Sorting Algorithm and Searching Algorithm, techniques, their implementation, advantages, and coding analysis

Pre-requisite: JavaScript

Theme

Amazon heavily relies on searching and sorting algorithms to provide efficient search and sorting functionalities for its vast product catalog. When customers search for specific items, Amazon's search algorithm quickly retrieves relevant results, considering factors like relevance, popularity, and customer ratings. Additionally, sorting algorithms are used to present search results based on criteria like price, customer reviews, and availability, allowing users to find and compare products easily.

Primary Goals

  • To introduce the fundamental concepts and techniques used in sorting and searching algorithms.
  • To provide an understanding of the different types of sorting algorithms and searching algorithms and their relative strengths and weaknesses.
  • Analyzing the time and space complexity of various searching and sorting algorithms.
  • To gain hands-on experience implementing and applying sorting and searching algorithms to real-world problems.
